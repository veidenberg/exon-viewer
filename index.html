The breast cancer type 2 susceptibility protein (encoded by the BRCA2 gene) is a vital tumor suppressor protein. Its primary function is to repair damaged DNA, particularly via homologous recombination. Inherited mutations in this gene significantly increase the lifetime risk of developing breast, ovarian, and other cancers.
